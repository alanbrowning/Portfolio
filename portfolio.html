Professional Projects

Here is a list of some of my professional engineering projects. They mainly consist of work I've completed during my co-op at Covestro. Note, I cannot disclose results or images of many of these projects.

Covestro Headlamp

Designed and simulated a headlamp containing multiple electronic components. Click here for more info.

Buick Headlamp Assembly

Analyzed a headlamp for future Buick model. Evaluated the feasibility of polycarbonate within the assembly. Made design recommendations to optimize thermal performance using polycarbonate within the headlamp. Click here for more info.

Nasa Space Suit (Briefs)

Analyzed viability of Covestro resins for future NASA space suits. Compared the performance of polycarbonates and composites based on given loadings. Click here for more info.

Ford Fog Light and Mounting Bracket

Performed thermal and vibrational analyses to determine the viability of polycarbonates in foglights as well as headlamp brackets for future Ford F-150s. Recommended design changes to optimize maximum stresses and temperatures. Performed topological optimization to future improve the design. Click here for more info.

Thermal Staking Research

Designed tensile testing fixtures to characterize performance of thermal staking between dissimilar polycarbonates. Click here for more info.

Re-think the Rink

Determined the performance of a newly proposed board design. Analyzed a real-life NHL collision to quantify the effects of the new boards. Click here for more info.

Integrated Reflector

Performed hundreds of CFD simulations to feed through neural network to predict the effects of changes in dimensions of a heatsink/reflector. Click here for more info.
